## YOLOv11 Training Dataset


The dataset can be obtained from Zenodo:

https://zenodo.org/records/20149614

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In yaml, classes `0` to `2` represent three different users. Classes `3` to `7` represent obstacles or reflectors with different materials.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The dataset is used for training YOLOv11, as shown in the figures below. It includes images and the corresponding labels.

The labels contain both bounding box information and keypoint information. Although most labels have been checked, we cannot guarantee that all annotations are perfectly accurate or tightly marked. Users may need to further verify or refine the labels depending on their specific applications.
